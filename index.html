"How It Works" and "Key Features" Sections of Box Collaboration

I was asked to pick one part of my initial design and flesh it out how I would like to see it. I chose to further develop the "How It Works" section and the "Key Features" section. I wanted to highlight each feature as a section that would take a full page rather than a column in a crowded section (note:previous design). By allowing a full page, the reader/user is allowed to focus on each key idea and digest the features one at a time.

In addition, I redesigned all of the images that Box presented on their site to match Chartbeat color scheme. I wanted to make them a bit more flat and fun then they were on Box. I believe this makes them more digestible and better represented of a high level idea such as "Cloud Project Collaboration".

How It Works: Project Collaboration

Box Business and Box Enterprise make it easy to store and collaborate on project documents in a single online location. Result: You streamline any workflow in a just a few clicks.

Keep Everyone in the Loop

When you create project folders and sub-folders, everyone's on the same page.

Good-bye Inbox, Hello Collaboration

Confusing email exchanges, endlessly searching for crucial updates; with Box, the in-box is out. Now your team has a central place to share, edit, discuss and approve content.

Collaboration on Your Desktop

Box Sync automatically keep multiple folders from your Box account in sync with a folder on your desktop. Your whole team can do the same, so edits and updates appear on everyone's desktop.
